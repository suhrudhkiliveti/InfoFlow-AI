IT Support Knowledge Base - Test Document

Password Reset Instructions:
1. Go to the company login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your company email address
4. Check your email for reset instructions
5. Follow the link and create a new password

VPN Setup Guide:
1. Download the company VPN client from the IT portal
2. Install the software on your device
3. Use your company credentials to connect
4. Select the appropriate server location
5. Test your connection by accessing internal resources

WiFi Troubleshooting:
1. Check if WiFi is enabled on your device
2. Restart your router and modem
3. Forget and reconnect to the network
4. Update your network drivers
5. Contact IT if issues persist

Software Installation:
1. All software requests must go through IT
2. Submit a ticket with software requirements
3. IT will verify licensing and security
4. Installation will be scheduled within 2 business days
5. Training materials will be provided if needed
